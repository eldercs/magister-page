In the last class, I decided to connect my life with programming, because that he believed that the profession had great potential in the modern world, because of which he passed the exam in mathematics, very successfully.

Professional development

I began to think about the choice of the future profession in the 8th grade. Initially, I planned to connect my life with such sciences as biology and history. In 10th grade, I rethought my decision and decided become an engineer, like my parents. I saw in this recruiting popularity directional great potential in the future and also I loved work with a computer and wanted to learn more about the secrets of this technology because of which I decided to join.

During my studies, I studied many different technologies and worked with many programming languages. I tried to make applications in C++, C# and Java, drawing in Photoshop, creating neural networks in Python, doing applications for mobile devices in Android Studio and studied working with SQL databases using C# and PHP, but I liked the most web development because of my love for design and drawing. Also more than once had the opportunity to participate in group projects and work on various interesting projects. During my studies, I had the opportunity to work trainee at two enterprises and get acquainted with their structure. After studying specialties, I decided to further develop in the field of front-end developer.

At the moment I am already working in the field of web development on a beginner position and improve my skills. I create pages with using Vue 3 and sometimes modifying the frontend part in PHP.

The main plan for the future is the defense of a master's thesis, graduation university, employment in Russia for the specialty frontend a developer who will be close to home and become good at it specialist. Among personal goals, I would like to highlight the improvement of skills English, especially spoken.

In the long term, I plan to gain enough experience to become more qualified and visit other countries and work there.
